Authentic Algerian Chakchouka

A simple, vibrant, and spicy tomato-based skillet dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 Onion, chopped
  • 1 Red Bell Pepper, chopped
  • 2 Garlic Cloves, minced
  • 400g Canned Tomatoes
  • 1 tsp Cumin
  • 1 tsp Paprika
  • 4 Eggs
  • 1 Handful Parsley

Instructions

  1. Sauté: Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the onion and bell pepper. Cook for 5 minutes until the onion becomes translucent.
  2. Simmer: Stir in garlic and spices. Pour in the canned tomatoes. Reduce heat and simmer for 10-15 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  3. Poach: Use a spoon to make small wells in the sauce. Crack an egg into each well. Cover the pan and cook for 5-8 minutes until eggs are done to your liking.
  4. Serve: Garnish with fresh parsley and serve immediately with crusty bread.
